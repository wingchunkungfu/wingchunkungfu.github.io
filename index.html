Wing Chun
The purpose of our association is to preserve and promote Grand Master Yuen Chai Wan's Wing Chun as passed down to him during the late 1800s from Imperial Constable Fok Bo Chuen. We hope to ensure that the system is passed onto future generations and not lost. We are based across the world China, Thailand, Vietnam and Australia. Contact us here.
